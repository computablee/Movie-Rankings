Lane's Ultimate Movie Ranking List

About the List

These are simply my personal opinions based on my personal taste in movies. I claim in no way to be objective in how I rank movies, nor do I have a specific list of criteria upon which I decide where in this list a movie should go. This list is largely constructed via "feel." How I feel about the movie after watching it and how much personal enjoyment I got out of the movie is almost entirely the basis upon which I will rank it.

With that said, here is my list. I am currently in the process of adding movies, and many of them I will need to rewatch to get an accurate judgment. Please be patient as I work through nearly every movie I've ever seen and quantify a rating!
